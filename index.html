What's this?

With this advanced tool you can create and share your own monochrome sprites. Design your sprite by clicking the pixels and then click "Tweet" or "Copy" (for other social platforms).

The sprite editor uses modern Unicode technology to achieve what once was only possible to do with a video game and a telly.
